About Me

David was born in Denver, Colorado in 1954 and his family moved to the Eatonville area in 1962. His mother and five siblings also live in Eatonville and they have over 70 relatives within a one hour drive. David has two children. Tim lives in the Yelm area (three kids) and Ben lives in Eatonville (two kids). David has earned a Bachelors degree in Economics, an equivalency Bachelors in Business Administration and a Masters in Business Administration. David is also a Master Lean Trainer (efficiency expert) and is Six Sigma Black Belt certified (analysis expert). He has owned multiple businesses through the years and is currently co-owner of Christensen’s Home Furnishings with his son Tim and working on the opening of Yogi’s Smokeshop with his son Ben. David has also been buying and remodeling houses his whole life and has significant construction expertise.

Qualifications

The entirety of David’s experiences to this point makes him uniquely qualified to evaluate and improve business structures and processes (which the town of Eatonville is in dire need of). As Mayor, David will restructure the current town organization, reset town goals, reset employee expectations and behaviors, evaluate and improve all town processes (thereby streamlining response times) and focus on improving the current Eatonville Municipal Code and planning department services. The citizens of Eatonville will experience improved interactions with town employees at all levels.

Goals

  • Restructure the town organization (full time Public Works Director and Planner, no Town Administrator)
  • Analyze and improve town processes and procedures
  • Develop a town vision/mission statement
  • Reset town goals/objectives and town staff focus
  • Implement a five year strategic plan
  • Rewrite current town code (minimize and simplify)
  • Promote investor friendly business growth
  • Aggressively pursue annexation opportunities
  • Develop a town theme that pulls the town together and includes the airport and nearby parks
